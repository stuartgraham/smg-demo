How classes work

  • Open to anyone aged 18 or over
  • Three terms aligned to the school year: Term 1 (12 weeks, Sep), Term 2 (10 weeks, Jan), Term 3 (8 weeks, Apr)
  • You should have an instrument to bring to class
  • Learning by ear: Tutors teach slowly at a pace suitable for the group. Bring a recording device. Music is also shared via photocopies or cloud storage.
  • Read our Class FAQ for answers to common questions

Membership & fees

  • Annual membership: £20 (£15 concession)
  • Membership gives a discount on class fees
  • Purchase membership before buying classes to receive the discount
  • Membership is optional — runs 1 Aug to 31 Jul
  • Save money with Early Bird rates each term
  • Concession rates available for students, pensioners and those on low income

Ready to get started? Term 3 Early Bird pricing ends 7th April.

Monthly Events

Ceilidhs

Our ceilidhs are lively, friendly, informal and fun for all ages. You'll be made welcome whether you come on your own or with a party. You don't need to know what to do – a caller will explain the dances and there are lots of friendly folk to give you a hand and get everyone up dancing.

Our ceilidhs often sell out – please buy tickets in advance to be sure of entry!
